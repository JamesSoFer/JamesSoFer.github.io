About projects

During my studies as a web developer, I completed several projects that deepened my skills in JavaScript, React, etc. I began by building a personal portfolio website, and websites using a dynamic frontend applications for handling interactive content.

webwave

webWave is a personal project where I am focused on developing minimalist websites that adhere to the principles of minimalism while creating a significant impact. The goal is to help entrepreneurs and businesses successfully launch their websites with clean, effective designs that stand out in today's crowded online space.

By prioritizing simplicity and clarity, WebWave ensures that each website effectively conveys the brand's message and values. This approach not only enhances the user experience but also helps drive better engagement and conversions.

By empowering entrepreneurs and businesses with well-designed, impactful websites, WebWave aims to support them in establishing a strong online presence and achieving success in their respective industries.
